# Avengers Maze - Gameplay Guide

Welcome to Avengers Maze! Navigate through a fog-covered maze, defeat villains, and find the exit to claim victory.

## How to Play

### 1. Select Your Hero

Choose from four Marvel heroes, each with unique abilities:

| Hero | Health | Attack | Vision | Special Abilities |
|------|--------|--------|--------|-------------------|
| **Hulk** | 150 | 25 | 2 | Wall Smash, Ground Pound |
| **Falcon** | 100 | 18 | 4 | Recon, Glide |
| **Hawkeye** | 90 | 22 | 3 | Trick Arrow, Eagle Eye |
| **Stretch** | 85 | 15 | 3 | Squeeze, Long Reach |

### 2. Navigate the Maze

- Use **Arrow Keys** or **WASD** to move
- The maze is shrouded in fog - explore to reveal tiles
- Your **Vision** stat determines how far you can see

### 4. Combat

When you step on an enemy tile, combat begins:

- **Attack** - Deal normal damage
- **Special Attack** - Deal 1.5x damage

Combat is **simultaneous** - both you and the enemy deal damage each round. It's possible for both to fall in the same round!
